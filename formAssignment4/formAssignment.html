About the Marks Calculator

This Marks Calculator is used to calculate the Final Exam mark of a student. It takes in input of two test marks - test Mark 1 and test Mark 2, compares them and gets the best done of the two. A coursework mark out of 100 is input, that is added to the best test Mark and an average of the two calculated, then converted to 40%. This gives the general Coursework Mark. Then an exam mark out of 100 is input, converted to 60% and then added to the general Course Work Mark. This gives the Final Exam mark for the student.
